![image](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/d8ff0eb7-8cda-47ff-bfab-69ce7804d08a)## Android教程

#### 1.应用程式下载，选择一个网盘下载  
- [蓝奏云端网盘](https://tagcloud.lanzouw.com/iokrd2hcglmb)
- [小飞机网盘](https://share.feijipan.com/s/cjDro9sD)  

---

#### 2.安装并打开APP，安装完成后，打开Clash Meta for Android，应用图标如下  
![教学图](https://storage.crisp.chat/users/helpdesk/website/de54da2065412800/image_i0qxs6.png)  

---

#### 3.添加新的配置：在主界面，点击“配置”  
![教学图](https://storage.crisp.chat/users/helpdesk/website/de54da2065412800/photo2024-12-0612-27-16_svjoc6.jpg)  

---

#### 4.然后点击右上角的“➕”号，选择“URL”从 URL 导入  
![教学图](https://storage.crisp.chat/users/helpdesk/website/de54da2065412800/photo2024-12-0612-33-22_q8b404.jpg)  

---

#### 5.添加您购买的订阅网址，订阅网址请跟客服索取  
#### 配置订阅信息：在“名称”处填写“雷速”，在“URL”处粘贴刚刚复制的订阅链接，然后点击右上角的“保存”按钮
![教学图](https://storage.crisp.chat/users/helpdesk/website/de54da2065412800/photo2024-12-0612-36-58_1nzzc54.jpg)  

---

#### 6.选择订阅，点击圆圈选择刚添加的订阅，选中后会有圆点反馈，然后点击左上角箭头返回主界面  
![教学图](https://storage.crisp.chat/users/helpdesk/website/de54da2065412800/photo2024-12-0612-56-37_ekvxdw.jpg)  

---

#### 7.启动服务：点击“已停止，点此启动”启动软件。出现网络连接请求提示时，必须点击“确定”  
![教学图](https://storage.crisp.chat/users/helpdesk/website/de54da2065412800/photo2024-12-0613-01-15_7tvsbr.jpg)  

---

#### 8.选择节点：点击“代理”选择节点  
![教学图](https://storage.crisp.chat/users/helpdesk/website/de54da2065412800/photo2024-12-0613-11-45_1vqwf69.jpg)  

---

#### 9.选中喜欢的节点后会有深色选中反馈
![教学图](https://storage.crisp.chat/users/helpdesk/website/de54da2065412800/photo2024-12-0613-15-44_66zt6p.jpg)
